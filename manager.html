Manager

His friendly personality allows him to maintain a positive environment where players feel confident and focused. Bijay plays a crucial role in organizing strategies, handling team coordination, and supporting the overall growth of ORIGINAL Esports. With his dedication, leadership, and commitment, he continues to push the team forward, helping them improve step by step and move closer to dominating the esports scene.
