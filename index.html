About ORIGINAL

ORIGINAL Esports is more than just a gaming team — it represents a dream built through passion, discipline, and ambition. Founded with the goal of representing Nepal on the global esports stage, the team focuses on competitive excellence in Free Fire and PUBG. From humble beginnings, ORIGINAL has grown step by step through dedication, teamwork, and continuous improvement. Every match, every challenge, and every experience is part of a bigger journey — to rise slowly and dominate completely.
