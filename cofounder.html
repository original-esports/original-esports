Co Founder

Rushal Darlami Magar, the co-founder of ORIGINAL Esports, is a 19-year-old passionate gamer currently living in Tokyo, Japan. As an experienced Free Fire player, he brings both skill and strategic thinking into the team. Rushal is known for his smooth gameplay style, combined with moments of aggressive performance when needed. His ability to stay calm under pressure while delivering strong in-game decisions makes him a valuable part of the team. As a co-founder, he plays an important role in shaping team strategies, supporting players, and helping the organization grow. His dedication to improvement and teamwork reflects the core values of ORIGINAL Esports — discipline, consistency, and the hunger to dominate.
