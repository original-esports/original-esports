Founder / CEO

Diamond Thapa, the founder and CEO of ORIGINAL Esports, is a 20-year-old esports enthusiast currently living in Tokyo, Japan. Starting as a passionate Free Fire player, Diamond transformed his love for gaming into a bigger vision — building a competitive esports organization that represents Nepal globally. Beyond gaming, he is also a growing YouTuber with over 29.3K subscribers, where he shares gameplay, experiences, and content with his audience. His journey reflects dedication, consistency, and a strong mindset focused on improvement and success. Diamond leads ORIGINAL Esports with discipline and ambition, aiming to take the team to international levels while proudly representing Nepal. His leadership style is built on growth, teamwork, and the belief of rising step by step toward domination.
