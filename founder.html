Founder / CEO

Diamond Thapa, the founder of ORIGINAL Esports, is a 20-year-old esports enthusiast currently living in Tokyo, Japan. As a passionate gamer and former Free Fire player, he has built his journey from casual gaming into a competitive esports vision. Beyond gaming, Diamond is also a YouTuber with over 29.3K subscribers, where he shares his experiences, gameplay, and community content. His leadership is driven by discipline, ambition, and a clear goal — to take ORIGINAL Esports to an international level while representing Nepal proudly. His journey reflects consistency, self-growth, and the mindset of rising step by step toward domination.
